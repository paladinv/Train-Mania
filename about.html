About Train Mania

Train Mania is a bright train-routing puzzle game inspired by the spirit of classic pipe-laying games. Place track pieces quickly, beat the countdown, and guide your little train safely to the destination.

What is included

  • Custom game logo and in-game SVG sprites.
  • Tutorial-first game flow with increasing round difficulty.
  • Persistent browser leaderboard for top scores.
  • Achievement popup progression between rounds.
